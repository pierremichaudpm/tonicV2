About Groupe Tonic

For over 40 years, we've been creating unforgettable moments by organizing major international sporting and cultural events in Quebec.

Our Mission

Groupe Tonic is a creator of memorable experiences. We organize and produce major sporting and cultural events that bring communities together and celebrate excellence. Our expertise extends from conception to execution, including promotion and complete management of large-scale events.

With a passionate team and strong partnerships, we transform every project into a unique experience that leaves a lasting impression and creates enduring memories for participants, spectators, and communities.

Our History

A little trip back in time

1978-2000

1978
Creation of Serdy Vidéo
The beginning of our adventure in the world of audiovisual production.
1979
The 1st Marathon de Montréal
A family story begins with the organization of the first Marathon de Montréal.
1988 to 1992
Grand Prix des Amériques
Organization of the prestigious Grand Prix des Amériques.
1992 & 1994
Olympic Games
Production of the Barcelona 1992 and Lillehammer 1994 Olympic Games for TVA.
1999
Tour Trans-Canada
Organization of the Tour Trans-Canada.
2000
Évasion
Launch of Évasion (Tour de France broadcasting rights).

2010-Today

2010
Launch of Zeste and IDHD
Creation of the GPCQM under the Serdy group.
2013
Zeste and Espaces Magazines
Launch of Zeste and Espaces magazines.
2017
Launch of DOCK 619
Opening of our unique event venue.
2019
Sale to TVA
Sale of Serdy to TVA. The GPCQM remain the property of Sébastien Arsenault.
2020
UCI Road World Championships 2026
Submission of candidacy to host the UCI Road World Championships 2026 in Montréal. Obtaining the Marathon de Montréal organization mandate.
2022
Marathon Beneva de Montréal
First edition of Marathon Beneva de Montréal under the Courons Montréal banner. UCI Road World Championships 2026 awarded to Montréal by the organization.
2024
21K de Montréal
Acquisition of 21K de Montréal which joins the MBM under the Courons Montréal banner.
2025
Volleyball World Beach Pro Tour
Acquisition of Volleyball World Beach Pro Tour Élite 16 Montréal. Resumption of DOCK 619 management, a high-end event hall for corporate and private events.
