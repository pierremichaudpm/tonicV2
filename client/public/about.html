About Tonic

For over 40 years, we've been creating unforgettable moments by organizing major international sporting and cultural events in Quebec.

Our Mission

Tonic is a creator of memorable experiences. We organize and produce major sporting and cultural events that bring communities together and celebrate excellence. Our expertise spans from conception to execution, including promotion and complete management of large-scale events.

With a passionate team and strong partnerships, we transform every project into a unique experience that leaves a lasting impression and creates enduring memories for participants, spectators, and communities.

Our History

1982 - Foundation

Creation of Tonic with the vision of transforming Quebec's event landscape.

1985 - First Cycling Grand Prix

Launch of the Quebec and Montreal Cycling Grand Prix, quickly becoming must-attend events.

2003 - Montreal Marathon

Taking over and revitalizing the Montreal Marathon, now one of Canada's most important.

2022 - Beach Pro Tour

Hosting the Beach Pro Tour, confirming Montreal as a major destination for world beach volleyball.

2026 - UCI World Championships

Preparing to host the UCI Road World Championships, a historic event for cycling.
