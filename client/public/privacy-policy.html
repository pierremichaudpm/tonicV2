Privacy Policy

Protection and use of your personal data

Last updated: July 22, 2025

Groupe Tonic respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy informs you about how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information Collection

We collect the following types of information:

  • Information you provide directly to us (name, email, phone)
  • Website navigation and usage information
  • Technical data (IP address, browser type, pages visited)

2. Use of Data

We use your data to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and communications
  • Improve our services and website
  • Inform you about our events and services (with your consent)
  • Comply with our legal obligations

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information only in the following cases:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • With our service providers (under confidentiality agreement)

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, destruction, or disclosure.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access and rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. You can configure your browser to refuse cookies, but this may affect the functionality of the site.

7. Contact

For any questions regarding this privacy policy or to exercise your rights, contact us:

Groupe Tonic
Email: info@tonicproductions.ca
Address: Longueuil, QC, Canada

8. Changes

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. We will notify you of any significant changes by email or through our website.
